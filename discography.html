Rather than being buried in endless negative thoughts in bad situations, Seventeen understands youth conflicts and wandering from a healthy and positive perspective, saying, "Let's not get tired and overcome instead of being pessimistic and frustrated on our own."The album will include a total of six songs, including the double title songs "F*ck My Life" and "Son Goku," each unit song "Fire" (hip hop team), "I Don't Understand But I Luv U" (performance team), and the group song "April Shower." In particular, S.Coups, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Mingyu, Vernon, and Dino, led by album producer Woozi, will participate in writing or composing and present high-quality music.
